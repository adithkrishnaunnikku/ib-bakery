Our Story

A Legacy of Taste in Thodupuzha

Indian bakery has a long bound history of 45 years. It has been founded by Mr. V. T. Joseph. Located conveniently in Thodupuzha town, we blend traditional Kerala recipes with modern baking techniques to bring you unparalleled flavor in every bite.

From our melt-in-the-mouth Plum Cakes to our crispy evening puffs and legendary faloodas, every item is crafted with love and the finest ingredients.
